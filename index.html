About Me

Hello! I’m Eleonora, a professional translator from German and English into Italian.


In 2019, after completing my master’s degree in Milan, I moved to Berlin, where I started my career as an in-house translator for e-commerce platforms. Since then, I’ve translated all sorts of digital and marketing texts: from FAQ sections to blog articles, social media content to meta tags, you name it, I've (probably) translated it.


Since going freelance in 2023, I regularly translate marketing materials, website content and business texts for direct and agency clients in the fields of tourism, luxury watches and lifestyle.


I have an informal and friendly approach with my clients, and I aim to create regular and long-lasting collaborations.
