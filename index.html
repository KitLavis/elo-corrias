About Me

Hello! I’m Eleonora, a professional Italian translator from English and German.


After completing my degree in Milan in 2019, I moved to Berlin, where I started my career as an in-house translator. Since then, I’ve translated all sorts of digital texts - from FAQ sections to blog articles, from social media content to meta tags, from product descriptions to blog articles, you name it.


I’ve been freelancing since 2023. I collaborate regularly with direct and agency clients in the fields of tourism, luxury watches, lifestyle and well-being. Given the nature of my specialisms, I have a touch for texts that require a certain degree of creativity and cultural sensitivity - such as blog articles, newsletters, social media content, etc.


I have an informal and friendly approach with my clients, and I aim at creating regular and long-lasting collaborations.
